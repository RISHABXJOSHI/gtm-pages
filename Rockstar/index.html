3 campaign ideas I would test for Rockstar Games

These are not final strategies. They are examples of how I think through sourcing, signals, and outreach angles.

1
Homepage & Newswire

Grand Theft Auto VI Pre-Order Campaign

Source
Homepage carousel and newswire announcements.
Signal
Pre-orders begin on June 25, 2026, with a dedicated "Learn More" CTA already live on the site.
Why it works
The anticipation for a major franchise release is already high. Early purchase incentives combined with exclusive content create a natural urgency that converts interest into commitment before launch day.
Outreach angle
Promote pre-order bonuses and exclusive content to incentivize early purchases across email, launcher notifications, and social channels.
2
Newswire & GTA Online Section

GTA Online In-Game Event Promotions

Source
Newswire articles and the "Jump Into GTA Online" section on the homepage.
Signal
Regular updates on new content like "A Safehouse in the Hills" and "The Next Big Score" are being published consistently.
Why it works
Fresh content keeps the existing player base engaged and gives lapsed players a reason to return. Limited-time rewards create a fear of missing out that drives re-engagement.
Outreach angle
Highlight limited-time rewards and new gameplay features through automated email sequences and launcher notifications to drive re-engagement.
3
Rockstar Store Section

Rockstar Games Store Merchandise Push

Source
Dedicated "Store" section on the homepage with prominent links to new arrivals, apparel, collectibles, and accessories.
Signal
The store is prominently featured with clear navigation paths for different product categories.
Why it works
Brand loyalty extends beyond the screen. Physical goods let fans carry the experience into their daily lives. New merchandise drops and limited-edition items create urgency and give collectors a reason to check back regularly.
Outreach angle
Feature new merchandise drops and limited-edition items through email campaigns and targeted social ads to create urgency and drive store traffic.

How I would support the team

My role would be to handle the research and workflow building so senior teams can focus on creative direction and strategy.

Define the campaign objective and audience segment For example: GTA VI pre-order prospects, lapsed GTA Online players, or merchandise buyers segmented by franchise affinity.
Research signals and behavioral triggers Find the events, platform activity, purchase history, content engagement patterns, and seasonal moments that indicate readiness to act.
Build and enrich the audience list Use the signal criteria, enrich key fields, map personas, and document why each segment fits the campaign objective.
Create a campaign research doc Include audience definition, signal sources, timing rationale, offer angle, sample messaging, potential objections, and enrichment notes.
Hand it to creative and marketing teams The team can refine the angle, approve the audience, and launch without starting from a blank page.
